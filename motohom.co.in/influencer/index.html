Welcome to Motohom Caravans

About us

At Motohom Caravans, we’re passionate about redefining the way people explore the world. Founded with the vision of bringing unparalleled comfort and innovation to the travel industry, we specialize in designing and manufacturing custom-built caravans that cater to every traveler’s unique needs.

With years of expertise in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted name for high-quality caravan manufacturing. Whether you’re looking for a luxurious mobile home for family vacations, a compact caravan for off-road adventures, or a tailored design for commercial use, Motohom is your ultimate partner in creating travel solutions that are as functional as they are elegant.

Our Mission

To inspire a sense of freedom and adventure by crafting caravans that combine cutting-edge technology, world-class craftsmanship, and exceptional comfort.

Our Vision

To become the leading name in caravan manufacturing, fostering a community of travellers who embrace innovation, sustainability, and the joy of discovery.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Tailored Designs: Every caravan we create is meticulously designed to suit the lifestyle, preferences, and requirements of our clients.
  • Uncompromising Quality: From premium materials to advanced engineering, we ensure that every detail of your caravan meets the highest standards.
  • Eco-Friendly Practices: We are committed to sustainability and incorporate eco-conscious methods and materials in our manufacturing process.
  • Strong After-Sales Support: Our journey with you doesn’t end at delivery; we provide ongoing support to ensure your caravan remains in top-notch condition.

A Journey Built on Collaboration

At Motohom, we believe that the best creations come from understanding our clients’ dreams. Our team of skilled designers, engineers, and craftsmen work closely with you to transform your vision into a reality. From the initial consultation to the final touches, we ensure every step of the process reflects your personality and travel goals.

Our Achievements

  • -- Successfully delivered a diverse range of caravans, from compact adventure models to luxury mobile homes.
  • -- Collaborated with industry leaders and tourism partners, including initiatives like promoting Maharashtra tourism.

Experience the Motohom Difference

At Motohom Caravans, we don’t just build caravans – we create memories, opportunities, and experiences that last a lifetime. Step into a world where adventure meets comfort and every journey becomes extraordinary.
